7 New Targets UK & EU Markets Confidential March 2026

Strategic Context

Building on eToro Money's existing acquisition pipeline (Emma, Plum, thinkmoney, Pockit, Meniga, Dreams, Yolt and others), this supplementary research surfaces additional UK and EU consumer fintech targets that can accelerate the SuperApp roadmap. The focus is on companies with proven credit products, open banking infrastructure, AI-driven financial management, and engaged consumer bases that would complement eToro's existing investing platform and deepen daily financial utility for users.
